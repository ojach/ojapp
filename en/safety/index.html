Safety Guidelines

1. URL Transparency

Before redirecting, OJapp always displays the destination URL exactly as it is.
You can clearly confirm where you are being redirected, providing peace of mind when sharing links.

2. HTTPS Verification

OJapp checks whether the entered URL uses HTTPS (secure encrypted communication).
If the URL may be unsafe, a warning will be shown.

3. External Safety Check (Coming Soon)

We plan to integrate external security services such as the Google Safe Browsing API.
This will help detect phishing, malware, and other threats.
Verified links will display a “Safe Badge”.

4. No Ads, No Tracking

OJapp displays no advertisements and does not track any user behavior.
We do not collect personal information.

5. Safe Auto-Redirect

Countdown timers and redirects only start after a user intentionally opens the generated page.
They cannot be triggered externally or automatically.

This page is provided to help you use OJapp with confidence.
